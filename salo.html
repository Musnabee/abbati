The Challenge

Nigerian fashion creatives face significant barriers to establishing professional online presence. Traditional website development is expensive, complex, and requires technical skills that most designers and tailors don't possess.

Additionally, managing clients, projects, and income often involves juggling multiple tools, spreadsheets, and manual processes that eat into valuable creative time.

Our Solution

SALO provides completely free, professional brand landing pages that require no coding skills. Paired with KraftOS, our integrated business management dashboard, creatives get everything they need in one place.

This democratizes access to professional digital tools, allowing talented creatives to focus on what they do best while technology handles the business infrastructure.

Platform Features

Brand Landing Pages

Create stunning, mobile-friendly landing pages that showcase your unique story, portfolio, and connect directly with potential clients. No coding required, just your creativity and vision.

KraftOS Management

Comprehensive business dashboard to track clients, manage work orders, handle invoicing, and monitor income streams. Available immediately after account creation.

Mobile-First Design

All landing pages are optimized for mobile viewing, ensuring your brand looks professional on any device your clients use to discover you.

Business Growth Tools

Integrated analytics, client inquiry tracking, and growth insights help you understand your business performance and identify opportunities.

Seamless Integration

Connect your brand page to WhatsApp for instant client communication and integrate payment solutions for smooth transaction processing.

Community Support

Join a growing community of fashion creatives sharing insights, collaboration opportunities, and mutual support for business growth.
