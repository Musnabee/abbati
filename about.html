About Me

The journey from engineering to entrepreneurship

Building Businesses That Bridge Worlds

With a Mechanical Engineering degree from Ahmadu Bello University and over seven years of hands-on business experience, I've evolved from understanding how machines work to understanding how markets work.

My journey began in retail at Inusa's Haberdashery, where I learned the fundamentals of sales, procurement, and customer relationships. From Sales Representative to Store Manager to Procurement Officer, I gained invaluable insights into what makes businesses tick at the ground level.

Today, I apply that systematic, engineering approach to building diverse business ventures that serve real market needs - from helping tailors and seamstresses through Sewcart, to empowering small businesses with digital tools via Salo, to revolutionizing content creation with AI assistance.

My Philosophy

Business success comes from understanding real problems and building systematic solutions. Whether it's managing inventory in a retail store or developing digital platforms, the principles remain the same: listen to your market, deliver value consistently, and never stop innovating.

My Entrepreneurial Journey

2017 - 2019

Foundation in Retail

Started as Sales Representative at Inusa's Haberdashery, learning customer service, product knowledge, and the fundamentals of retail operations. Consistently achieved sales targets while building strong customer relationships.

2020 - 2021

Leadership & Operations

Promoted to Store Manager, leading sales teams and implementing strategies that increased store sales. Developed expertise in inventory management, team leadership, and customer relationship building.

2022

Academic Achievement

Graduated with Bachelor of Engineering in Mechanical Engineering from ABU Zaria, combining technical knowledge with practical business experience.

2023 - 2024

Strategic Procurement

Advanced to Sales and Procurement Officer, mastering vendor negotiations, supply chain management, and strategic planning. Earned certifications in entrepreneurship and warehouse management.

2024 - Present

Multi-Venture Entrepreneur

Launched Sewcart retail business, developed Salo digital platform, established Magazine.Salo, and pioneered AI-assisted publishing. Building an ecosystem of interconnected business ventures.
